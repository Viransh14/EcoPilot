# 🌱 EcoPilot

- Know your carbon number. Actually change it.

EcoPilot is an AI-powered sustainability coach that helps users understand their environmental impact and take practical steps to reduce it.

Most carbon footprint calculators stop after showing a number. EcoPilot goes further by turning sustainability insights into personalized missions, progress tracking, achievements, reports, and AI-guided recommendations that help users build long-term eco-friendly habits.

Built for PromptWars, EcoPilot combines carbon footprint analysis with behavioral change systems to make sustainability measurable, actionable, and engaging.

## Live Demo

**Website:** https://ecopilot.netlify.app/

## Problem

Many people care about sustainability but struggle with questions like:

* How large is my carbon footprint?
* Which habits contribute the most to it?
* What actions should I take first?
* How do I stay consistent over time?

Traditional carbon calculators often provide information without helping users act on it.

## Solution

EcoPilot acts as a personal sustainability companion.

Users complete a footprint assessment covering transportation, home energy usage, food habits, and consumption patterns. EcoPilot then calculates an EcoScore, estimates annual carbon emissions, identifies high-impact areas, and recommends personalized sustainability missions.

The platform also includes progress tracking, achievement systems, streaks, reports, and an AI sustainability coach that provides contextual guidance based on the user's footprint profile.
